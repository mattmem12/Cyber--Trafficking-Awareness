Hotlines for Support

If you or someone you know is a victim of human trafficking, please seek help immediately:

  • National Human Trafficking Hotline: 1-888-373-7888
  • Text Line: Text "HELP" to 233733 for immediate assistance.
  • Local Law Enforcement: Call 911 in an emergency.
